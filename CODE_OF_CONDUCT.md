# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on technical collaboration.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Disruptive or abusive conduct.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, comments, or contributions that violate this policy.
Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues via the security contact route in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
